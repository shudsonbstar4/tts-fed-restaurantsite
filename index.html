About Us

The new all-American dining experience

Located in the heart of downtown Charlotte, NC, Restaurant Site is proud to offer the freshest, most savory dining experience you'll find in the Queen City. From burgers to steaks to succulent desserts, we're proud to offer a wide variety of items on our menu. Our cozy downtown location is open daily for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
